Cats

September 22, 2016

Did you know that cats are an ecological disaster! These invasive predators live in the artificial shelters of humans roam the wildness killing large numbers of birds and rodents. This has caused the extinction of over 33 different birds. They are also one of the few animals the kill for fun, freaking metal man. This has caused many birds to refer to them as "The Heartless Fur" or in bird "Soosoososososo".
