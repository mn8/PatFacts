About Pat

Patrick Coady combines his enthusiasm, eccentricity, and breadth of knowledge to bring you a new fact every work day. While not coming up with facts, he spends his free time playing Rocket League and playing with VR. A software developer by day, Pat is sure to keep you both enlightened and entertained at the same time.

About the site

PatFacts is a specialized microblogging platform designed to deliver one fact a day to its userbase. It is developed and designed from the ground up by Patrick Coady and Joe DiFebo, allowing for a maximum level of customization for both Pat and its users. Development on the site is ongoing to constantly push the boundaries of fact-spreading and knowledge-sharing to as many people as possible. The site was established in September 2016.
